The humble To-Do list, a tool as ancient as human civilization, has evolved remarkably over millennia. From the clay tablets of the Sumerians to the notebooks of Renaissance thinkers like Leonardo da Vinci, the practice of jotting down tasks has always been integral to organizing our lives. In modern times, digital apps have revolutionized To-Do lists, adding features like reminders, collaboration, and synchronization across devices. Despite these advancements, the core purpose remains the same: to help us remember, prioritize, and achieve our goals efficiently.
